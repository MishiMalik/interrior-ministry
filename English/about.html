The Ministry of Interior is one of the oldest state agencies, established in 1932 to promote various developmental aspects across the regions. It is responsible for proposing the general policies of the state regarding governorates and local affairs and ensuring their implementation within the framework of applicable laws and royal decrees. The Ministry also prepares relevant research and studies, works to strengthen the fundamental principles and authentic values on which Omani society is based, and fosters its unity and cohesion. Additionally, it coordinates with governorates and other relevant authorities to protect the interests of citizens. The Ministry oversees issues related to the administrative division of the Sultanate of Oman, defines its geographic scope, addresses matters related to international borders and boundary agreements, and ensures their implementation in coordination with relevant bodies. Furthermore, it manages issues concerning Omani nationality, organizes tribal affairs, and addresses related matters across all governorates.

The Ministry also plays a prominent role in organizing the election of members of the Shura Council and municipal councils, overseeing the electoral register, as well as supervising and monitoring municipal councils in accordance with applicable laws and royal decrees. Additionally, it manages the affairs of reconciliation and mediation committees, overseeing and monitoring their performance.

Royal Decree No. 37/2022
Specifying the Functions of the Ministry of Interior and Approving Its Organizational Structure

We, Haitham bin Tariq, Sultan of Oman,

After reviewing the Basic Statute of the State,
and the Administrative Apparatus of the State issued by Royal Decree No. 75/2020, and Royal Decree No. 102/2020 specifying the functions of the Ministry of Interior and approving its organizational structure,and the Governorates System issued by Royal Decree No. 36/2022,and based on the requirements of public interest,

we decree as follows:

Article One

The functions of the Ministry of Interior shall be specified according to the attached Appendix No. (1).

Article Two

The organizational structure of the Ministry of Interior shall be approved according to the attached Appendix No. (2).

Article Three

Royal Decree No. 102/2020 and any provisions that contradict this decree are hereby repealed.

Article Four

This decree shall be published in the Official Gazette and shall come into force from the date of its issuance.

Issued on:

16th of Dhul-Qi'dah, 1443 AH,

Corresponding to:

June 16, 2022

Haitham bin Tariq

Sultan of Oman
